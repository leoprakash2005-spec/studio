# **App Name**: RentEase India

## Core Features:

- OTP Authentication: Enable users to securely log in using OTP-based authentication.
- Role-Based Access: Allow users to select their role (Landlord or Tenant) after OTP login, and store the role in Firestore for access control.
- Rent Due Notifications: Generate and send scheduled notifications to tenants, powered by a LLM tool, reminding them of upcoming and overdue rent payments, based on rules about proximity to the due date and/or overdue status.
- Razorpay Payment Integration: Integrate Razorpay payment gateway to allow tenants to pay rent online via UPI, cards, or net banking.
- Payment History Tracking: Maintain payment history logs with details such as amount paid, transaction ID, payment date, and payment method.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant orange (#FF9800) to evoke feelings of trust and energy.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), providing a soft, neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Warm red-orange (#FF4500) to highlight crucial CTAs.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif), offering a balance of modernity and personality.
- Utilize simple, outlined icons that relate to property management and payments.
- Maintain a clean and well-organized layout, providing an easy mobile experience with clear hierarchy for data.
- Subtle animations during transactions.
